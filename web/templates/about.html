About Multimodal Lie Detector

Project Overview

The Multimodal Lie Detector is an advanced AI-powered behavioral analysis system that combines multiple modalities to assess behavioral consistency and detect potential deception indicators.

Instead of relying on a single modality, our system synthesizes insights from facial expressions, eye movements, speech patterns, and acoustic characteristics to provide a comprehensive analysis.

Key Features

👁️ Advanced Face Analysis

Detects micro-expressions, face movements, and subtle facial cues that may indicate deception

🔍 Eye Movement Tracking

Monitors gaze patterns, pupil dilation, blink rates, and fixation duration

🎵 Audio Pattern Analysis

Analyzes pitch variations, speech rate, pauses, and emotional tone

🧠 AI-Powered Reasoning

Uses Google Gemini for advanced multi-step reasoning on multimodal data

📊 Comprehensive Reporting

Generates detailed analysis reports with visualizations and recommendations

🔐 Privacy Focused

Secure processing with automatic deletion of uploaded files after analysis

Important Disclaimer

This system is designed for research and analytical purposes only. The analysis results should not be used as the sole basis for critical decisions. Behavioral analysis is complex and influenced by many factors.

Results should be interpreted by qualified professionals in conjunction with other evidence and investigative methods.
